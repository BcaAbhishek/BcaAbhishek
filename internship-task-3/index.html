About Biryani

Biryani is a celebrated and aromatic mixed-rice dish from South Asia, known for its complex layers of flavor and rich history. A festive meal often associated with royalty, it features fragrant, long-grain rice (most commonly basmati) cooked with meat, seafood, or vegetables and a distinctive blend of spices.
