About Bengali Dishes

Bengali cuisine centers on rice and freshwater fish, using mustard oil, poppy seeds, and panch phoron to create distinct sweet, sour, spicy, and savory flavors. Staples include various rice and fish dishes like Sorshe Ilish (Hilsa in mustard paste) and Chingri Malai Curry (prawns in coconut milk), alongside vegetarian favorites such as Aloo Posto. The cuisine also features a wide array of sweetmeats and desserts like Sandesh and Mishti Doi, often made with khejur gur (date palm syrup)
